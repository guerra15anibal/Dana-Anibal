October 9th, 2021!

How we met

When people ask us “how we met..” your mother, sorry we love that show. We met through mutual friends of friends. I lived in Alamosa and he lived in Monte Vista. I was given Anibal’s phone number before actually meeting him face to face. After a few months of talking on the phone and exchanging messages we decided to meet. He took the drive about 20 minutes away to come see me.

Something that even took him by surprise! We were both very nervous! I remember thinking “What if this guy abducts me? “ But after 10 years he is the one who can probably say I abducted him! After meeting we both knew we liked each other as more than friends. I was happy the yelling, he thought was my dad, at the park we met at didn’t scare him away! We was still very nervous even after meeting so we eased into things. He would get rides to come see me everyday and when he got his license and bought a car he would drive and stay every weekend or when he could at my place.

We took things very steadily and unlike what most couples did at the time in our area we decided not to transfer schools and to do things “long” distance! Once we graduated and finished college we moved in together in our current place in Alamosa. We got our two fur babies and cannot wait to get our own house someday! We both are so blessed that we met when we did. Like they say the right people come into your life at the right time. For us both that couldn’t be more true.

The Proposal

So after 7 years of being together I finally decided to pop the question. It was March 17, 2017 two days after my birthday, you could say I saved the best present for last. I was planning it for a while with my friend Luis, I'd give him the ring to wrap up as one of my presents for my birthday. Every year he wraps my birthday present like some sort of drug package! So it is pretty difficult to get into she didn't suspect a thing.

She was recording me trying to open it, but this thing was layered like an onion! I started to get so nervous the closer I got the weaker I started to feel. My heart was beating so fast I finally made to the ring box, she still had no idea! Until I got on one knee, her jaw literally dropped she flipped the camera on herself and said, "See guys I knew I would record my own proposal!" It was an amazing time, I'm so grateful to have this lady in my life she means everything to me. From my girlfriend to my fiancee and soon to be wife!

Pagosa Springs, Colorado

Pagosa Springs is a town in southwest Colorado known for its hot springs. It’s surrounded by the rugged San Juan Mountains and vast areas of national forest. The San Juan River, with its abundant trout, runs through town. To the northeast, Wolf Creek Ski Area offers downhill runs and cross-country trails. Southwest, Chimney Rock National Monument features the ruins of ancient Ancestral Puebloan dwellings.

FAQs

Q: What is your COVID-19 contingency plan?

A. We plan to get married on October 9th, 2021 no matter what. We have a vision for our wedding day that we hope to see play out, but at the end of the day, the health and safety of our guests is of the utmost importance. While we hope that we will be able to see your smiling faces without face masks, we will also be sure to provide them if necessary based on the situation this fall. Our greatest wish is to celebrate our wedding safely and responsibly. We all must do our part to ensure that is exactly what happens. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.


Q: Are children allowed?

A. Though we love our nieces, nephews, little cousins and tiny friends, we have chosen to keep this an ADULT ONLY celebration. Thank you for understanding.


Q: Can I bring a guest?

A. If your invitation calls for an additional guest then yes, if not, it should just be you. We do have COVID 19 restrictions and a tight budget. There will only be room for those outlined on your invitation. Please do not bring any additional guests or dates as they will not have a seat.


Q: Is there a shuttle to and from the hotel to the venue?

A. Unfortunately, no. The hotel has stopped their shuttle service due to COVID-19. We suggest using UBER or Lyft as it would be a short 15 minute ride to the venue.


Q: Will We Need To Pay For Parking?

A. No. There is parking available at the venue but is limited, please carpool if that is an option.
